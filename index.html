The Problem

Most lead gen problems happen after the click.

More ad spend will not fix a broken lead system. If calls are missed, landing pages are generic, and follow-up is slow, your campaigns are doing their job but the system is not.

Leak 01

Broad keywords, wrong intent

Campaigns running on broad match attract researchers and job seekers, not homeowners ready to book.

Wasted spend

Leak 02

Traffic sent to the homepage

A homepage built for exploration, not conversion. Service-specific clicks need service-specific pages.

Lost intent

Leak 03

Calls not tracked or attributed

60 to 80 percent of home services conversions happen by phone. Without call tracking, you are optimizing blind.

No visibility

Leak 04

Slow or no follow-up

Leads that do not get a response within minutes go cold. Most companies have no automated follow-up at all.

Revenue lost

Leak 05

No attribution to booked jobs

Without connecting ad spend to actual booked jobs, budget decisions are guesswork.

Unknown ROI

Leak 06

No operating rhythm

Campaigns set and forgotten. No weekly review, no bid logic, no creative testing, no continuous improvement.

Decay over time

What VonClaro Fixes

Six parts of the system. All of them matter.

01

Campaign Structure

Rebuild around high-intent keywords for each service and service area. Exact and phrase match only. Negative keyword lists that grow weekly.

02

Landing Pages

Dedicated pages for each campaign that match the ad promise, load fast on mobile, and have one clear path to a call or estimate request.

03

Tracking and Attribution

Call tracking on every touchpoint. Form conversion events that fire correctly. Attribution from click to call to booked job.

04

Call and Form Conversion

Call extensions with forwarding numbers. Dynamic number insertion on landing pages. Forms that submit correctly to your CRM.

05

Follow-Up and Routing

Automated SMS within minutes of every inbound lead. Routing rules that get the right lead to the right person. No estimate request goes cold.

06

Reporting and Optimization

Weekly account reviews. Monthly reporting on cost per booked job by campaign. Continuous bid, creative, and budget optimization.

What Typically Improves

Common patterns when the system is fixed.

These are patterns we see consistently across home services accounts. Results vary by market, budget, and starting point.

Campaign Structure

Wasted spend drops when broad match is replaced with intent-based keywords

  • Irrelevant search terms blocked by negative keyword lists
  • Cost per click decreases as Quality Scores improve
  • Budget concentrates on searches with actual purchase intent

Landing Pages

Conversion rate increases when traffic lands on purpose-built pages

  • Service-specific pages outperform homepages by a wide margin
  • Mobile-optimized pages with single CTAs reduce drop-off
  • Cost per lead falls as the same spend converts more visitors

Tracking

Visibility into which ads are generating booked jobs, not just clicks

  • Call tracking reveals which campaigns drive real inbound volume
  • Smart Bidding improves when fed accurate conversion signal
  • Budget reallocation becomes data-driven instead of guesswork

Follow-Up

More estimates booked when lead response is fast and automatic

  • Automated SMS within minutes of every inbound lead
  • Fewer leads go cold while crews are on job sites
  • Higher booking rate from the same lead volume
